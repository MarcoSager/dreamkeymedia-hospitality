Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Who We Are

Dreamkey Media is a content, marketing, and automation agency based in Bangkok, Thailand. This page explains what information we collect on this website and how we use it.

Information We Collect

When you submit our "Apply Today" form, we collect the information you provide directly: your name, business name, email address, phone number, and what you're interested in.

We also use cookies and similar technology to collect basic analytics: pages visited, general location (country/city level), device type, and how you arrived at the site. This is collected automatically through Google Analytics and Meta Pixel.

How We Use It

Form submissions are used only to follow up with you about the services you enquired about. We do not sell or rent your information to anyone.

Analytics data is used to understand how people use this site and to measure whether our advertising is reaching the right audience.

Third Parties We Use

  • WhatsApp — when you apply, your enquiry summary opens in WhatsApp so we can respond directly.
  • Formspree — securely relays your form submission to our email.
  • Calendly — if you book a strategy call, Calendly handles scheduling.
  • Cloudflare Turnstile — a spam/bot check on our form, similar to a CAPTCHA.
  • Google Analytics & Meta Pixel — anonymous usage analytics and ad performance measurement.

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

Cookies

This site uses cookies for analytics purposes as described above. You can decline non-essential cookies using the banner shown on your first visit, or block cookies entirely through your browser settings.

Data Retention & Your Rights

We keep form submissions only as long as needed to respond to your enquiry and for our own records. You can request that we delete your information at any time by contacting us below.
